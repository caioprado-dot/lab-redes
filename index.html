About Barra dos Coqueiros IXPs

The Internet Exchange Points (IXPs) in Barra dos Coqueiros offer local networks the opportunity to exchange traffic directly, improving speed, reducing latency, and lowering costs.

Features of Barra dos Coqueiros IXPs

High-Speed Connectivity

Our IXPs provide lightning-fast speeds, connecting local networks to international networks.

Low Latency

Experience minimal delays with direct peering between networks.

Cost-Efficient

Reduce data exchange costs by eliminating third-party transit providers.
